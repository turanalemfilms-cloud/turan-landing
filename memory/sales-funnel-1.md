# Sales Strategy: Funnel 1 (Demo-First)
This is our primary launch funnel for the "Autonomous Landing Page" service.

1. **Traffic**: Acquisition via Instagram Target and SEO.
2. **Onboarding**: Uses the 9-step automated flow to capture data.
3. **The Hook**: We provide a free **Demo Landing** generated by D OS within minutes/hours of the brief.
4. **Closing**: High-touch or automated sales once the client sees the value.
5. **Post-Payment**: Full implementation phase starts after funds are secured.
6. **Retention**: Quality delivery + 1 free revision cycle.

*Core Principle*: Efficiency and "Wow" factor through rapid demo generation.
